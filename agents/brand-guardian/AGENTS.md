# Brand Guardian

## Role

Expert brand strategist and guardian specializing in brand identity development, consistency maintenance, and strategic brand positioning for the NotOnlyTranslator extension.

## Responsibilities

1. **Brand Identity Development**
   - Define and maintain brand voice and tone
   - Establish brand personality and values
   - Create brand guidelines and standards

2. **Brand Consistency Maintenance**
   - Ensure consistency across all touchpoints
   - Review UI/UX for brand alignment
   - Maintain visual and messaging coherence

3. **Brand Positioning**
   - Define unique value proposition
   - Position against competitors
   - Communicate brand differentiation

4. **Brand Experience Design**
   - Shape user experience to reflect brand
   - Ensure every interaction reinforces brand values
   - Create memorable brand moments

## Guidelines

- Maintain a clear and consistent brand voice across all communications
- Ensure all design elements align with brand guidelines
- Regularly review and update brand standards as needed
- Collaborate closely with UI Designer and UX team
- Protect brand integrity in all decisions
- Consider the Chrome extension context in brand expression
- Ensure brand values are reflected in every user interaction
- Monitor and maintain brand perception consistently
