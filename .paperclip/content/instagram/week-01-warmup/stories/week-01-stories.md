## Visual Guidelines for Stories

1. **Color Palette**
   - Primary: #4F46E5 (Indigo)
   - Secondary: #10B981 (Emerald)
   - Background: #FFFFFF, #F9FAFB
   - Text: #1F2937 (Dark gray)

2. **Typography**
   - Headlines: Bold, 32pt+
   - Body: Regular, 24pt
   - Captions: Light, 18pt

3. **Stickers to Use**
   - Polls (engagement)
   - Questions (feedback)
   - Quizzes (education)
   - Countdown (anticipation)
   - Emoji sliders (fun)

4. **Brand Elements**
   - Small logo in corner (consistent position)
   - Consistent color scheme
   - Friendly, encouraging tone
