## Hashtag Best Practices

### Do's ✅
- Always include #notonlytranslator (branded)
- Mix volume tiers (1-2 mega, 3-4 large, 4-6 medium, 3-5 niche)
- Rotate sets to avoid looking spammy
- Research trending hashtags weekly
- Create a unique branded hashtag challenge (future)

### Don'ts ❌
- Don't use the same 30 hashtags on every post
- Don't use banned or restricted hashtags
- Don't use irrelevant hashtags just for reach
- Don't exceed 30 hashtags (Instagram limit)
- Don't put hashtags in the first comment (less effective now)
