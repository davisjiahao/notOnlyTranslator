---

## Video Script

### Scene 1: Hook (0-3 seconds)

**Visual:** Person looking frustrated at computer screen
**Text Overlay:** "Stop translating every word you read"
**Audio:** Trending "Oh no" sound or attention-grabbing beat

**Action:** Quick cut to black screen with white text

---

### Scene 2: The Problem (3-10 seconds)

**Visual:** Screen recording showing traditional translation extension
**Text Overlay:** "❌ Full translation = you learn nothing"

**Voiceover:**
> "You've been reading English wrong your whole life. Full translation feels helpful, but your brain checks out. You're not learning, you're just... reading translations."

**Visual Elements:**
- Show a webpage with full translation overlay
- Brain icon with "sleeping" animation
- Red X marks over translated text

---

### Scene 3: The Solution (10-20 seconds)

**Visual:** Switch to NotOnlyTranslator interface
**Text Overlay:** "✅ Only translate what you don't know"

**Voiceover:**
> "NotOnlyTranslator is different. It only shows translations for words above your level. Everything else stays in English. Your brain stays engaged. You actually learn."

**Visual Elements:**
- Before/after split screen
- Same webpage, now with selective highlighting
- Green check marks
- Vocabulary level selector animation

**Screen Recording Details:**
- Show installation from Chrome Web Store
- Navigate to English article
- Demo: Click word → translation appears → click again → marked as known
- Show how known words stop being highlighted

---

### Scene 4: The Result (20-25 seconds)

**Visual:** Person smiling, reading naturally
**Text Overlay:** "Read naturally. Learn faster."

**Voiceover:**
> "Read like a native. Learn vocabulary naturally. No more translation dependency."

**Visual Elements:**
- Side-by-side comparison:
  - Left: "Before" - frustrated, switching tabs
  - Right: "After" - smooth reading with NOT
- Progress bar animation: "Vocabulary learned: +47 words this week"

---

### Scene 5: CTA (25-30 seconds)

**Visual:** Phone showing TikTok profile with link highlighted
**Text Overlay:** "Link in bio → Get it free"

**Voiceover:**
> "Link in bio. Free download. Start reading smarter today."

**End Cards:**
- Logo watermark
- "Follow for daily English tips"
- Point down gesture to bio link

---
