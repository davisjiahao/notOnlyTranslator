## Post Copy

"Your translation app might be hurting your English more than helping it.

I know—that sounds counterintuitive. But hear me out.

For years, we've been told that translating everything is the path to fluency. Just look up every word, memorize the definitions, and eventually you'll 'get it.'

Except... that's not how language acquisition works.

The research is clear: when you translate everything, your brain checks out. There's no struggle, no inference, no actual learning. You're just matching words.

Here are 4 myths about full translation that might be holding you back 👇

Swipe through to see the facts ➡️

The alternative? Selective translation—only translating words you actually need help with. That's the principle behind NotOnlyTranslator.

Have you noticed translation dependency in your own learning? Drop a 🙋 if this resonates.
