## Overview

Carousel posts are multi-slide content pieces that drive high engagement across Instagram, LinkedIn, and TikTok. Each carousel consists of 5-10 slides that tell a story, educate, or entertain through sequential visuals.

**Why Carousels Work:**
- Higher engagement rates than single-image posts
- Algorithm favors content with high "save" rates
- Perfect for educational content and storytelling
- Allows complex ideas to be broken down simply

---

## Carousel Formats

### Format 1: Educational Slide Deck (7-10 slides)
**Purpose**: Teach a concept related to English learning

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Hook (bold statement or question)
- Slide 2: Problem identification
- Slides 3-7: Step-by-step explanation
- Slide 8: Solution showcase
- Slide 9: Call-to-action
- Slide 10: Brand watermark

### Format 2: Listicle (5-7 slides)
**Purpose**: Quick, scannable tips

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: "X Things You Need to Know About [Topic]"
- Slides 2-6: Individual tips with icons
- Slide 7: CTA + download link

### Format 3: Before/After Transformation (5 slides)
**Purpose**: Show the impact of using NotOnlyTranslator

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: The struggle (before)
- Slide 2: The realization
- Slide 3: The solution
- Slide 4: The result (after)
- Slide 5: CTA

### Format 4: Myth vs Fact (6 slides)
**Purpose**: Challenge misconceptions about language learning

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: "Myths About Learning English"
- Slides 2-5: Myth | Fact pairs
- Slide 6: CTA

### Format 5: Quote/Story Carousel (5-7 slides)
**Purpose**: Emotional connection and relatability

**Structure:**
- Slide 1: Opening scene
- Slides 2-5: Story progression
- Slide 6: The lesson
- Slide 7: CTA

## Design Guidelines

### Visual Style

**Color Palette:**
- Primary: #3B82F6 (Trust Blue)
- Secondary: #10B981 (Growth Green)
- Accent: #F59E0B (Attention Amber)
- Background: #FFFFFF or #F8FAFC
- Text: #1F2937 (Dark Gray)

**Typography:**
- Headlines: Bold, 48-72pt
- Body: Regular, 24-32pt
- Captions: Light, 18-24pt

**Visual Elements:**
- Consistent branding on every slide
- Icons from Lucide or similar set
- Screenshots with device mockups
- Data visualizations where relevant
- Plenty of whitespace

### Slide Dimensions

| Platform | Dimensions | Aspect Ratio |
|----------|------------|--------------|
| Instagram | 1080x1350 | 4:5 (portrait) |
| LinkedIn | 1200x1200 | 1:1 (square) |
| TikTok | 1080x1920 | 9:16 (vertical) |

---

## Copy Guidelines

### Slide 1 (Cover) Rules
- Maximum 8 words for headline
- Must include a visual hook
- Clear preview of value inside

### Body Slides Rules
- Maximum 20 words per slide
- One idea per slide
- Use bullet points sparingly
- Include visual hierarchy

### Final Slide Rules
- Always include CTA
- Link in bio/bio link tool
- Brand logo/watermark

## Posting Strategy

### Best Times for Carousels

| Platform | Best Day | Best Time | Frequency |
|----------|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Instagram | Tue, Wed, Thu | 11am, 2pm, 7pm | 3-4x/week |
| LinkedIn | Tue, Wed | 8am, 12pm | 2-3x/week |
| TikTok | Any | 7pm-10pm | 2-3x/week |

### Hashtag Strategy

**Instagram:**
- 20-30 hashtags per post
- Mix: 5 broad + 15 niche + 5 branded

**LinkedIn:**
- 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Focus on professional tags

**TikTok:**
- 3-5 hashtags in caption
- Include trending sounds

## Content Production Workflow

1. **Ideation** (Monday): Review content calendar, select themes
2. **Scripting** (Tuesday): Write slide-by-slide copy
3. **Design** (Wednesday): Create visuals in Figma/Canva
4. **Review** (Thursday): Internal review and revisions
5. **Schedule** (Friday): Upload to scheduling tool
6. **Publish** (Scheduled): Auto-post at optimal times
7. **Analyze** (Weekly): Review metrics, iterate
