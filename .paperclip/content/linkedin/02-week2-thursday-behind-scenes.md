### Post Content

```
6 months ago, I was frustrated.

Every English article I read for work took 3x longer than it should.
Not because I couldn't understand English—I've been using it professionally for years.

But because the existing tools were broken:
• Full-page translation = reading Chinese, learning nothing
• Dictionary extensions = constant tab-switching, flow destroyed
• Vocabulary apps = completely disconnected from actual reading

So I started building something different.

NotOnlyTranslator only translates words above YOUR level.
It learns your vocabulary as you use it.
It keeps you in flow state while reading.

The beta feedback has been incredible.
People are reading 2x more content in the same time.
Vocabulary retention is up because words are learned in context.

Launching April 2 on Chrome and Edge.

For anyone who's struggled with English reading efficiency—this is for you.
```
